# X Thread

## Tweet 1

Built `DarkForest Briefs` with `@solrouter/sdk`: a private research CLI that turns a sensitive market question into a structured markdown brief.

## Tweet 2

Why private inference matters here: in trading or competitive research, the prompt is often the edge. If your raw thesis leaves your machine in plaintext, you already leaked the most valuable part.

## Tweet 3

This tool sends the query through SolRouter with encryption enabled by default, can use live search or BRAID reasoning, and exports a brief with thesis, catalysts, risks, missing evidence, watchlist, and execution summary.

## Tweet 4

I kept the code intentionally small so it is obvious where privacy is being enforced and easy for another builder to audit or extend.

## Tweet 5

If you are building anything around alpha, wallet automation, portfolio review, or internal research, private inference should be the default, not a premium add-on.
